Our Story

Over 137 years of refreshment, community, and the simple joy of sharing a Coke.

🥤

Born in Atlanta, 1886

It all started in a pharmacy in Atlanta, Georgia, where Dr. John Stith Pemberton created the syrup for Coca-Cola and carried a jug of it down the street to Jacobs' Pharmacy — where it sold for five cents a glass.

Since then, Coca-Cola has grown into one of the world's most recognized and beloved brands, served in over 200 countries and territories every single day.

We believe in the power of refreshment to bring people together — across cultures, languages, and generations.

1886 Coca-Cola invented by Dr. Pemberton in Atlanta, GA.
1915 The iconic contour bottle design is introduced.
1950 First TV advertisement airs, reaching millions.
1985 "New Coke" launches and Classic Coke returns — a defining moment.
2013 "Share a Coke" campaign personalizes bottles with names worldwide.
2024 Serving 2 billion drinks per day across 200+ countries.
